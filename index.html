Fun Facts About Jellyfish!

1. About half of jellyfish can glow in the dark!

2. Blooms are groups of jellyfish. Some of them even have millions of jellyfish!

3. They have no brain or heart.

4. While many jellyfish are translucent, some can come in different colours like bright red!

5. Jellyfish have been around for 500 million years, even before dinosaurs!

6. Jellyfish are 95% water.

7. The specialized cells that cause jellyfish stings are called nematocysts. They release harpoon-like needles to either defend themselves or catch prey.

8. Jellyfish are actually passive swimmers. Instead of actually swimming, they are normally pushed by the current.

9. Jellyfish aren't actually fish. They are more closely related to sea anemones and coral.

10. Some jellyfish are bioluminescent and can glow in the dark!
